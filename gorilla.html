ANIMALS MAP PLACES

GORILLA

Gorillas are gentle giants and display many human-like behaviors and emotions, such as laughter and sadness. In fact, gorillas share 98.3% of their genetic code with humans, making them our closest cousins after chimpanzees and bonobos.

The largest of the great apes, gorillas are stocky animals with broad chests and shoulders, large, human-like hands, and small eyes set into hairless faces.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

Gorillas typically live in lowland tropical rainforests of Central Africa
